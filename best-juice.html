Best Juice: 10 Best Juices We Tried in Our Taste Tests

Sometimes the best juice tastes like it was fresh pressed right before it arrived on the grocery store shelf. Other times, the best fruit juice is sweet and sugary and leaves a red stain above your lip. We, the juice-crazed Sporked staff, have tried them all. And now, we’ve listed all the winners of all of our best juice tastes tests here. 

From the best orange juice to the best fruit punch to the best tomato juice (we see you, tomato juice drinkers!), here are the best juice brands and the best juices they make that you can buy at the supermarket, no expensive, impossible-to-clean juicer required. 

Best Orangge Juice

Simply Orange Pulp-FreeTarget

Editor-in-chief Justine Sterling called this “the platonic ideal of store-bought orange juice.” It’s sweet and tangy and bright—perfect for gulping down at breakfast or mixing into a cocktail like a Mimosa or a Screwdriver. It’s not quite fresh-squeezed (there’s no pulp, after all) but it comes pretty darn close.

Best Cranberry Juice

Ocean Spray Cranberry Juice CocktailWalmart

Ocean Spray isn’t just the most prevalent cranberry juice, it’s also the best cranberry juice. “It’s a classic for a reason,” senior staff writer Jordan Myrick wrote in their ranking of the best cranberry juices. “It’s not too sweet and not too tart.” If you’re looking for the best fruit juice to drink straight or use as a mixer, this cranberry juice is a great option.

Best Tomato Juice

O Organics 100% Tomato JuiceVons

Sporked contributor Danny Palumbo called this tomato juice “refreshing” in his write-up of the best tomato juice brands. He praised how “natural” the juice tastes and noted that it has less salt and less sugar than its competitors. “It’s all tomato flavor, and I’m here for it,” he wrote. If you’re a tomato juice drinker (or you’re looking for a good base for a homemade Bloody Mary mix), this is one of the best fruit juices for you to have on hand (yes, tomato is a fruit for our purposes).
