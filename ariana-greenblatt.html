Ariana Greenblatt is an American actress born on August 27, 2007. She started to appear more frequently in the limelight after the role she played in the Disney Channel comedy series Stuck in the Middle (2016–2018) as Daphne.

Greenblatt showcased her acting skill in films such as A Bad Moms Christmas (2017), Avengers: Infinity War (2018), and In the Heights (2021).

Career

Her career continued to flourish in 2023 when she co-starred in the science-fiction film 65 where she played the role of Koa. Later played the role of Sasha in Barbie movie and young Ahsoka Tano in Ahsoka.

Personal Life

She is from a family deeply rooted in Broadway. Her father and paternal grandparents being Broadway producers. She is of Jewish descent on her father’s side, while her mother is of Puerto Rican descent.
