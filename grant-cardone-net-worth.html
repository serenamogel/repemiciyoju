Grant Cardone Net Worth: Business &amp; Lifestyle

An American motivational speaker, author, sales coach, and real estate investor, Grant Cardone’s net worth is a whopping $300 million as of December, 2023.

A sole founder of real estate investment agency Cardone Capital manages thirteen different business initiatives.

He also serves as the CEO of seven privately-held companies.

His primary business is Cardone Training Technologies which helps companies grow their sales by customizing the traditional sales procedures to be more effective and salable.

Mr. Cardone also owns and operates Cardone Acquisitions, Cardone University, and Grant Cardone TV Network.

Similarly, Cardone real estate currently owns over $2 billion worth of real estate across the United States.

However, Cardone has limited his skills to business ventures but has also authored several bestselling sales books.

To date, he has written eight business books.

These books have helped thousands of businesses worldwide to let go of their traditional and orthodox sales procedure and embrace the new one to generate more revenue.

The man is really busy serving as a CEO of numerous businesses and still manages to operate his social media handle.

Mr. Cardone teaches youths about sales, financial education, and entrepreneurship via his YouTube channel and other social media handles.

He also conducts webinars almost on a daily basis.

Grant Cardone was born on March 21th, 1958, in Lake Charles, Louisiana, United States.

He grew up with his four other siblings in Louisiana. He attended LaGrange High School in Lake Charles and graduated in 1976.

After high school, he went to McNeese State University and graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in accounting in 1981.

Cardone was awarded Distinguished Alumnus awards for outstanding graduates in 2010 by McNeese State University.
