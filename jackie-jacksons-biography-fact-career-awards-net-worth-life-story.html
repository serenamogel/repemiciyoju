Jackie Jacksons biography, fact, career, awards, net worth &amp; life story

  • Source of Wealth: Music
  • Age: 68
  • Birth Place: Gary, Indiana, United States
  • Marital Status: Married (Emily Besselink)
  • Full Name: Sigmund Esco Jackson
  • Nationality: American
  • Date of Birth: May 4, 1951
  • Ethnicity: African-American
  • Occupation: Singer, Musician
  • Children: 4 (River, Jaylen, Sigmund Jr., Brandi)

Born in may 4, 1951, this American musician is the second  child of the Jackson family and a member of the famous The Jackson 5. He has an estimated net worth of $200 thousand. At such a very young age, Jackie Jackson has developed an intense desire to become a professional baseball player someday. However, in 1964, he left his dreams behind and joined with his 4 younger brothers in forming The Jackson 5.

In Jackson 5, Jackie played the role of a falsetto singer. He occasionally played the tambourine during the group’s live performances. Despite the success of the group, Jackie Jackson ventured on a solo career when he recorded his self-titled album which featured his falsetto singing style. When his album did not succeed, he just continued working with the group whose popularity remained until the mid-70s.

Currently, Jackie is running two recording companies, the Futurist Entertainment and Jesco Records.  Apart from being a mentor to some new artists, Jackie also serves as the executive producer of the show, The Jacksons: A Family Dynasty.
