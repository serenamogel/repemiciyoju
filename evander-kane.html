Evander Kane- Net Worth, Age, Ethnicity, Wife, Height, Career

Evander Frank Kane, better known as Evander Kane, is a professional ice hockey player from Canada. He is an NHL left-winger for the San Jose Sharks. In the first round of the 2009 NHL Entry Draft, he was chosen by the Atlanta Thrashers. In 2007, he won the Memorial Cup with the Vancouver Giants of the WHL.

On the international stage, he led Team Canada to gold medals at the Ivan Hlinka Memorial Tournament in 2008 and the World Junior Championships in 2009. He also competed for Team Canada in the IIHF World Championships in 2010, 2011, 2012, and 2014.

Kane has dealt with a number of legal troubles throughout his professional career, including violence, gambling, and betting, among others. In 2021, his wife Anna accused him of betting on his own games, which was the most serious charge. The NHL has said that it will look into the allegations.

Evander Kane Biography

Evander Kane was born on the 2nd of August, 1991. He was born in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. He is a Canadian citizen. His father, Perry Kane, and mother, Sheri Kane, gave birth to him. He is of mixed ancestry (half African), and his faith is Christianity. He was raised in East Vancouver. Brea and Kyla are his two sisters.

In terms of schooling, he attended John Oliver Secondary School. Kane grew up playing baseball, basketball, ice hockey, and soccer. He then developed a strong interest in ice hockey. He played minor hockey for the Greater Vancouver Canadians and the Vancouver Thunderbirds of the BC Hockey Major Midget League.

Evander Kane Wife, Marriage

Evander Kane was married to Anna, who had a miscarriage in 2019 at 26 weeks. Eva was the name they had given to their unborn child. In July 2020, she gave birth to a daughter named Kensington. The couple divorced in 2021.

He was charged with assault following an incident in Vancouver in April 2014. He was later sued for financial restitution. In July 2016, a 21-year-old woman sued him for assault in a hotel room. In 2016, he was charged with one case of criminal trespassing and four charges of non-criminal harassment, to which he pled not guilty.

Kane was sued by the Cosmopolitan casino in Las Vegas in November 2019 after he defaulted on a $500,000 gambling debt. After accruing $26.2 million in debt, he filed for chapter 7 bankruptcy in California in January 2021.

His estranged wife, Anna, accused him of betting on his own team’s games. In an Instagram post, Anna accused Kane of “playing games with bookies to win money.” Kane refuted the allegations, saying, “I have NEVER gambled/bet on Hockey, a Sharks game, or any of my games.” He also revealed that his soon-to-be ex-wife suffers from mental illness. The NHL has said that it will look into the allegations against Kane.
