East Legend Panorama Hotel, Badamdar, Azerbaijan

East Legend Panorama Hotel has family friendly rooms that are well-spaced and are centrally air conditioned. High speed complimentary Wi-Fi access, flat screen TV, mini bar, tea & coffee making facilities.
