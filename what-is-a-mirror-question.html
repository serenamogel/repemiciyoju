What is a mirror interview?

Mirroring is an interview technique where you reflect your interviewer. Mirroring is not mimicking, it is adapting to the body language of your interviewer rather than copying them exactly. This method is used to establish coherence between you and your interviewer.

How do you ask probing questions in an interview?

Probing questions often begin with “what” or “how” because they invite more detail. Questions that begin with “Do you…” or “Are you…” invite personal reflection. “Why” questions can be problematic. They may put the respondent on the defensive or result in little useful information and require additional probing.
