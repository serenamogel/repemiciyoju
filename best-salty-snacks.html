Best Salty Snacks When a Craving Strikes

The best salty snacks are the best snacks, period. See, if you’re anything like me, then you know the truth: salty snacks are superior. To what, you ask? Well, I’d say most things in life. I’d take a free salty snack over free money nine times out of ten (the one exception being if the salty snack is pretzels and the money is more than one dollar). I know, a bold opinion. Hear me out, though: Have you ever met a problem that a potato chip, Cheeto, or chicken strip couldn’t make better? I can honestly say I have not. 

The best salty snacks are like a warm blanket on a cold winter’s day or an Otter Pop in the heat of the summer, EXCEPT that they are delightful in either of those situations and any situation in between. That being said, here are ten of the best salty snacks (in no particular order). Hopefully, you’ll find something new to try on this list of the best salty snacks—I know I certainly did.

Chex Mix Traditional

Chex Mix is one of the best salty snacks out there. It is varied, savory, and in my opinion, it is the Jeff Bezos of seasoning. That is to say, it’s LOADED. Plus, they brought the bagel chips back into the mix recently, which just elevates the already-pretty-perfect snack even higher on the snack food food chain. What makes Chex mix stand out among the many snack mixes out there in the snackaverse? Sporked managing editor Gwynedd Stuart put it best when she said that “it succeeds because seasoned Chex rule…they’re light and crispy and the seasoning really makes its way into little crannies in the cereal.” Turns out the shapes of the little mix components themselves really make or break a snack mix depending on how well the flavoring is able to stick to them. A cereal piece full of little tiny holes is the perfect seasoning catcher. If you haven’t revisited Chex Mix in a while, give it another go. The flavor is just so deliciously iconic. People might not use checks anymore, but I believe Chex will be around forever.

Chicas Lime Corn Tortilla Chips

I am a sucker for a nice, aggressively salty, significantly sour chip that actually tastes like limes. What can I say? Tostitos Hint of Lime made me who I am today. But we aren’t here to talk about Tostitos, we are here to talk about the lime-y, salty, greasy glory that is Chicas Lime Corn Tortilla chips. Gwynedd pointed out that “the quality of the chips themselves is so outrageously good.” They are oily and flaky and taste like they were just homemade in the fryer. On top of that, “they’re so salty and so limey that you’d really think they’d just been doused in fresh lime juice.” They literally taste like homemade chips with a pinch of salt and a squeeze of lime. They are ideal and definitely one of the best salty snacks to buy if you are looking for a sour, salty, greasy good time.

Related: The Best Lime Tortilla Chips

Stacy’s Simply Naked Pita Chips

Pita chips are one of those foods where you think they aren’t going to taste interesting enough, but then you eat an entire bag of them in one sitting and retroactively decide that they definitely deserve a place of honor in your pantry. You can dip these bad boys in anything from sweet (like Nutella) to savory (like hummus) to nothing at all and they are sure to satisfy that salty food craving. Believe us when we tell you that Stacy’s has got the title of “best pita chip brand” on lock. These Stacy’s chips purport to be plain, and yet, according to Gwynedd, “There’s no butter in the ingredients list, but they taste so buttery. There’s no garlic listed either, but I swear they have a hint of that savory goodness.” Is Stacy…a wizard? For a chip named after a dude from The Hunger Games, these are actually so good.

Related: Best Pita Chips: Grab the Hummus. We Found the 7 Best Pita Chips

David Bar-B-Q Jumbo Sunflower Seeds

I’ve said it before and I’ll say it again, I always feel scandalous when eating sunflower seeds since they were banned at my junior high school, but, hey, you know what they say about forbidden love. David’s Bar-B-Q sunflower seeds are the best salty snack for when you aren’t hungry but need something to do with your mouth (which, let’s face it, covers most snacking occasions). These bad boys are at the top of our best sunflower seeds ranking because they are “delightfully roasted and salty, and have a unique, simple flavor,” which Sporked contributor Danny Palumbo posited could either be from the roasting process or the quality of the seeds themselves. 

Related: Best Sunflower Seeds: Be a Hit in the Dugout with the 8 Best Sunflower Seeds

Deep River Mesquite BBQ Potato Chips

We’ve all been there. Sometimes what you really need is not a spa day, nor a massage, nor for every man in your life to say to you, “Actually, now that I think about it, you’re right!” Sometimes what you really need is simply a bag of BBQ potato chips. And let me tell ya, girlie, these Deep River Mesquite BBQ chips will do the trick for you. Sporked staff writer Jordan Myrick wrote that these chips have an “unimaginable amount of BBQ flavor” and are “so salty, sweet, smoky, and zesty—they truly tap every taste bud on your tongue,” and I think I would like that right now, please and thanks.

Related: Best BBQ Chips: Put Away Your Grill: These Are the 7 Best BBQ Chips

Deep River New York Spicy Dill Pickle Kettle Cooked Potato Chips

Knock knock. Who’s there? It’s Deep River again bringing us another absolutely bangin’ chip flavor to make our mouths smile will chippity goodness. These Spicy Dill Pickle Kettle Chips from Deep River Snacks are so good that they have literally become a part of Jordan’s daily routine. They described them as “crunchy, greasy, and dilly” and while we aren’t totally sure what the company means by “New York Style,” Jordan decided that it doesn’t really matter given the overwhelming quality of the chips themselves. Jordan also said they go great on sandwiches and with dips. 

Related: Best Dill Pickle Chips: The Best Dill Pickle Chips (We’re Talking the Potato Kind)

Takis Fuego

Ah, Takis. The chip so good I put it in my bio. The chip that is as intensely tasty as it is red. The real-life tangible love child of the abstract concepts of sour and spicy. The GOAT of all spicy chips. If you like spice and haven’t had a Taki yet, what are you waiting for, my guy? Do yourself a favor and try them. They taste like lime, spice, salt, MSG, and the color red got spray painted on a rolled corn chip (in the best possible way). Jordan described these as “not for the faint of heart, but they are for the strong of taste buds,” and I believe in you and your taste buds. If you want one of the best salty snacks, go eat some Takis!

Related: Best Takis Flavors: Let’s Talk About the Best Takis Flavors

Cheetos Crunchy Cheddar Jalapeño

At the risk of putting the nerdiest sentence ever out here on the internet, I first tried these Cheetos on a road trip with my college a cappella group. I was pleasantly surprised. They are not as spicy as Flamin’ Hot, but they do still have a kick, PLUS they have a huge cheesy flavor and a hint of jalapeño flavor. They are a VERY well-balanced salty snack, and one of the best salty snacks, period. Danny calls these “a flavor-blasted, flat-out-addictive snack product” and he is 100% right. He also said, “Where classic Cheetos lack spice, and Flamin’ Hot Cheetos lack cheese flavor, Cheddar Jalapeño Cheetos are the best of both worlds.” So if you are a cheesy gal but also a spicy gal, maybe give these a go the next time your salty snack cravings kick in.

Related: Best Cheetos Flavors: Orange Stained Fingers Be Damned, We Ranked the 9 Best Cheetos Flavors

Alexia Crispy Seasoned Potato Puffs

Now you may be thinking to yourself, “What on Beyoncé’s green earth is a potato puff?!” because I know that’s what I was thinking. Turns out they are another brand’s (Alexia’s) take on tater tots (Fun Fact: Ore-Ida owns the name/invented tater tots). But these aren’t just any tots, they are seasoned with “roasted garlic, cracked black pepper, and a hint of sour cream and chives,” and Gwynedd pointed out that the “garlic and cracked pepper flavors are bold and delicious,” and stood out to her. These seem like a great late-night, coming-back-from-a-party-and-not-ready-for-bed-but-a-100%-ready-for-fried-food kind of salty snack.

Related: Best Tater Tots: Fill Up Your Pockets with the 7 Best Tater Tots

Kraft Original Macaroni & Cheese Cups

Lastly, we’ve got these beauties. When you want a salty snack that also warms your bones and makes you feel like the world is giving you an ooey-gooey, cheesy hug, these mac and cheese cupsare the best salty snacks to buy. I lived on this stuff for at least a month sometime in my junior year of college. Not because I needed to, but because I wanted to (and also forgot to set aside time to cook). But as a snack, this is quite honestly one of the more comforting salty options out there. Jordan describes these mac and cheese cups (Easy Mac, if you’re in the know) as having “a mild, cheesy flavor, a creamy texture, and an overall sense of nostalgia,” which is so accurate. These taste like the warm glow of childhood. Am I being a bit melodramatic? Perhaps. Does that change the fact that these microwavable mac and cheese cups slap? Absolutely not. Go get one this week and treat yourself to one of the best salty snacks.
