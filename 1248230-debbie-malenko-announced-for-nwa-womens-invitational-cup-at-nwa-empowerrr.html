Debbie Malenko Announced For NWA Womens Invitational Cup At NWA Empowerrr

Debbie Malenko has been announced as an entrant in the NWA Women’s Invitational Cup at the promotion’s Empowerrr event. The winner of the battle royal will challenge for the NWA Women’s Championship at NWA 73. Chelsea Green, Jamie Senegal, Lady Frost and Tootie Lynn have also been announced for the match.

NWA shared the news in a new tweet that welcomed Malenko into the event.  

We're proud to announce, with her recent return to #prowrestling, @DebbieMalenko has entered the NWA Women's Invitational Cup!

Trained by the great Boris Malenko — from AJW to MMA, battling through injury and a major return, NOBODY demonstrates what #EMPOWERRR is about more! pic.twitter.com/i0dSSEW76s

— NWA (@nwa) August 9, 2021

Previously, Malenko competed for All Japan Women’s wrestling in the early 1990s. She held AJW Championship, but she had to vacate it due to an injury that essentially ended her in-ring career. But Malenko has recently returned to professional wreslting, and now she’s getting a shot at a major opportunity. Trained by the legendary Boris Malenko, Debbie is no stranger to big moments, and she will need to harness all that she has in order to come out on top at NWA Empowerrr.
