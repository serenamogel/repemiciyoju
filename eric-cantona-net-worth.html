Eric Cantona Net Worth: How Much Is Eric Cantona Worth?

Eric Cantona Net Worth: How Much Is Eric Cantona Worth?: Eric Cantona, also known as Eric Daniel Pierre Cantona is a French actor and former professional footballer.

As a footballer, Cantona was capable of playing as a center-forward, as an out-and-out striker, as an attacking midfielder, or as a central midfielder on occasion.

What Is Eric Cantona Net Worth?

As of October 2023, Eric Cantona has an estimated net worth of about $30 million. He has accrued such fortune from his career as an actor and a professional footballer (retired).

Cantona played for Auxerre, Martigues, Marseille, Bordeaux, Montpellier, Nîmes, and Leeds United before ending his career at Manchester United.

At Manchester United, he he won four Premier League titles in five years as well as two League and FA Cup Doubles.

Cantona is regarded as one of the greatest Manchester United players of all time and has legendary status at the club

He’s credited with having played a leading role in making Manchester United the dominant force in English football in the 1990s.

Cantona wore the iconic No. 7 shirt at Manchester United and was known for turning up his collar – a trademark look.

At international level, Cantona played for the France national team and scored 20 goals in 45 matches.

He appeared at UEFA Euro 1992 and ahead of Euro 96, he was appointed the nation’s captain until a football suspension from an incident in January 1995.

In 1997, Cantona unexpectedly announced his retirement from football at the age of 30. He pursued a career in cinema and had roles in several films.

His film credits include; Happiness Is in the Field, Elizabeth, The Children of the Marshland, The High Life, The Overeater, The Car Keys, It’s Our Life, The Second Wind, Jack Says, Looking for Eric, The Mad Kings, and Anka, among others

Cantona later took an interest in the sport of beach soccer; as player-manager of the France national team, he won the 2005 FIFA Beach Soccer World Cup.
