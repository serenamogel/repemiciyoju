Margot Robbie Barbie Makeup 2023

From strutting the red carpet to channeling supermodel Claudia Schiffer at the airport, Margot Robbie has consistently hit the fashion mark throughout the press run for the upcoming Barbie live-action film. Robbie has sported fun Barbiecore looks by Chanel, Versace, Bottega Venetta, and Moschino. With the help of her new stylist Andrew Mukamal, she also flexed her eye for accessories with a rotation of Manolo Blahnik mules and Barbie pink Steamline suitcases.

While I can't get enough of her many vintage Barbie doll fashion references, Robbie has taken an equally pink approach to her makeup over the last few weeks. Naturally, as a beauty editor who was obsessed with my childhood collection of Barbie dolls, I'm living for her fun and fresh makeup vibe—and it turns out you don't need to spend millions of dollars to look like a star.

Where to Buy

Ulta, Sephora, and Revolve

What is it?

A creamy multi-purpose bronzer stick with a soft-matte finish that can be applied to the face, eyes, and lips for a chic, monochromatic look.

How to Apply It Like Margot

Celebrity makeup artist Patti Dubroff shared with TikTokers the exact beauty products she used on Robbie during a Barbie event in Sydney. (More details in a bit.) After prepping Robbie's skin and brows, Dubroff used her finger to apply Nudestix NUDIES MATTE All Over Face Bronze Color in Sunkissed Cool, a bronze rose hue with cool undertones, and blended with a fluffy brush.

Pro Tip: Grab your foundation brush and use whatever coverup is left on the bristles to help soften the cheek's edges.

What Our Team Thinks

I've been covering beauty since 2016, and as someone who loves minimal, multipurpose products, I can confidently say this Margot Robbie-approved makeup product is totally worth incorporating into your beauty routine.

Getty Images

Beyond the array of shades to choose from, the easy-to-blend cream makeup is a breeze to apply. It's honestly goof-proof, so anyone just getting into makeup will find it easy to wear. Don't worry: Those with larger beauty collections will also enjoy swiping this onto their face, eyes, and lips.

As a bonus, the double-ended product includes a duo-fiber brush for blending. So if you have any upcoming summer travel plans, it's one less makeup product to stuff into your makeup bag.

I also love that Dubroff embraced my favorite beauty mantra: Makeup is fun and beauty rules are meant to be broken. Although this is labeled as a bronzer, Dubroff used it as a blush, and we can all take a page from her book. Get creative this summer (and beyond!) with the help of this affordable celebrity beauty product.

What Reviewers Say

To quote a 5-star Ulta review, "I bought this after reading good reviews. As an older woman in my 40s, I wanted something quick and easy (cheeks, eyes, and lips). This fits the bill and the color stays all day. As a bonus, the creamy texture doesn't smear off and stays throughout the day without being greasy and doesn't settle into fine lines. Also works great in the Texas heat!"

Other Celebrity Fans

Dubroff has been in the makeup game for decades and is likely your favorite celebrity's dream makeup artist. Case in point: Socialite Sofia Richie Grainge, daughter of music legend Lionel Richie and half-sister of reality TV star and fashion entrepreneur Nicole Richie, hired Dubroff to perfect her timeless yet fresh wedding glam.

During a post-wedding TikTok, Dubroff revealed that she layered Nudestix Nudies Matte Cream Blush in Sunkissed, a warmer-toned coral bronze, and Picante, a dainty coral, on Richie Grainge. The vibrant coral hue is still sold out, but Sunkissed is available to shop RN. Richie Grainge's wedding day makeup also inspired a slew of TikTokers, and Sofia recently joined the fun and recreated her viral wedding day makeup on TikTok.

More Products to Recreate Margot Robbie's Barbie Makeup

Curious about what other beauty products Dubrof used on Robbie? Same! In the same TikTok video, she continues that after prepping Margot's base and brows, she applied makeup from Byredo, Chanel, Makeup by Mario, DIBS Beauty, and Sunnies Face.
