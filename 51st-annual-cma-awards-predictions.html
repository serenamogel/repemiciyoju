Celeb Secrets Countrys Winner Predictions Celeb Secrets Country

We’re about two months out from “Country Music’s Biggest Night,” however, everyone at Celeb Secrets Country is already talking about who will be at the 51st Annual CMA Awards this year.

Announced on Good Morning America by Lauren Alaina, Dustin Lynch, and Brothers Osborne on Monday, it was revealed that Miranda Lambert tops the list of nominees with five nominations. Little Big Town and Keith Urban each garner four nominations, tying for the second most nominations this year. Eric Church, Maren Morris, Thomas Rhett and Chris Stapleton were also heavy hitters this season, as they each earned three nominations. You can view the full list of nominees here.

We must say, the competition is HOT this year. With so many talented artist in each category, it’ll be interesting to see who actually takes home the big W in November!

Click through the gallery to read our predictions and make sure to tune in to the 51st Annual CMA Awards LIVE from Nashville’s Bridgestone Arena on Wednesday, November 8th @ 8/7c on ABC to see who wins.
