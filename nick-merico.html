Who is Nick Merico?

Nick Merico was born on 29 December 1995 in Argentina as Nicolas Merico. He is the son of a mother, Cheryl Merico and father, Fredy Merico, soccer coach. In addition, Merico has one sister, Lexi Merico. As per his citizenship, he is Argentina, and ethnicity is not known.
