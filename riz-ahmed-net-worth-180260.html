Riz Ahmed Net Worth

Riz Ahmed is an English actor born on December 1, 1982. He is best known for his role as Bodhi Rook in the 2016 Star Wars anthology film Rogue One. He has also starred in other films such as Four Lions, Nightcrawler, and Jason Bourne. Additionally, he has had a successful career on television, most notably as Nasir Khan in the hit HBO miniseries The Night Of.
