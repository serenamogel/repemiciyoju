Chargestone Cavern - Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokmon encyclopedia

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

Not to be confused with Chargestone Cave.

The Chargestone Cavern (Japanese: 電気石の岩窟 Electric Stone Cavern) is a cavern within the Terarium, located in the Unova region.

Geography

Much like Chargestone Cave, Chargestone Cavern is full of blue, electrically-charged rock formations (which causes the player's Koraidon or Miraidon to become shrouded in bright blue sparks if they drive near one).

Places of interest

Rest area

A rest area can be found inside Chargestone Cavern, northwest from the main Canyon Biome entrance. Despite being deep inside the cave, Flying Taxis can be called to this location once visited. The player can heal their Pokémon here and purchase items from vending machines.
