Kevin Dias top movies, TV shows and awards

Paris was the place of Kevin Dias’ birth.

He made his acting debut at the age of 10 in Jean-Pierre Jeunet’s film Amélie. He studied acting at the renowned theatrical school Cours Florent after finishing high school. In the five seasons of the TF1 television program SAM, he portrayed Alex. His most recent English-language appearance was as Benoît in the Netflix series Emily in Paris.

Kevin Dias top movies and TV shows

Since he was little, Kevin has been acting. His acting resume includes roles in the 2001 film Amélie and the French TV series Sam and Clem.

On December 8, 2021, Dias revealed that he and filmmaker Fah Touahri collaborated on the short film Le Rôle de Ma Vie (The Role of a Lifetime). Robin Clive and his son Milo appear in the project, which is based on his life. Later, it made its debut on his social media.

Kevin also appeared in Emma Hoet’s “12h03” music video.
