Let’s get ready to RUUUMBLE!

In 2023, the WWE is hosting a slew of major events, from the annual Royal Rumble at San Antonio’s Alamodome on Jan. 28 to Monday Night Raw at Brooklyn’s Barclays Center on Feb. 13 to the Road to Wrestlemania at New York’s Madison Square Garden on March 12 all leading up to WrestleMania at Inglewood, CA’s SoFi Stadium on April 1 and April 2.

Major stars like Roman Reigns, Ronda Rousey, Charlotte Flair and Kevin Owens are rumored to enter the ring for a handful of bouts this year.

Even better, there’s a strong possibility you may even get to see influencer-turned-wrestler Logan Paul destroyed this year.

Who could pass that up?

Still need even more incentive to skip Pay Per View and see WWE live?

Here’s the scoop — some tickets are going for as low as $36 before fees on Vivid Seats.

Now, that’s a steal.
