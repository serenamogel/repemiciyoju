Celebrate 10/17 With A Classic Gucci Mane Playlist

October 17th is a special day for Gucci Mane. Any fan of the Atlanta Trap God will know he's has made the date the unofficial holiday of his 1017 Brick Squad movement, often dropping new music in celebration of the event. Today is the first 10/17 in 3 years that the rapper has spent the day outside of a jail cell, and he's dropped a new project, Woptober, 3 days in advance, as well as announced a brand new one (The Return Of East Atlanta Santa) in honor of the occasion.

In case there wasn't enough brand new Gucci material available to you, we've put together a selection of classics, deep cuts, and fresh anthems to bring in Gucci's big day. From the crushing bass of Chicken Talk's "Swing My Door" to the dancing keys and colorful wordplay of "Lemonade,' there should be something for every type of Guwop fan. Check out the full mix below, and let us know which tracks you're bumping today.
