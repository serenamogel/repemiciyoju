Easy Spicy Ranch Pretzels

These Spicy Ranch Pretzels are totally addicting and use only 4 ingredients! This delightfully easy recipe is sure to become a new family favorite! Perfect for game day!

Course Appetizer Cuisine American Keyword spicy pretzel Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Cook Time 30 minutes minutes Total Time 35 minutes minutes Servings 16 servings Calories 194kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

Ingredients

  • 16 oz mini pretzels
  • ⅔ cup extra light olive oil or canola oil
  • 1 tbsp crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 oz package ranch dressing or dip mix

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 300F.

  • Line a large, rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.

  • Combine oil, red pepper flakes, and ranch mix in a small bowl. Whisk together.

  • Place pretzels in a large bowl.

  • Drizzle pretzels with ranch mixture and toss with tongs until evenly coated.

  • Place coated pretzels on prepared baking sheet.

  • Bake for 30 minutes, stirring every 10 minutes.

  • Serve and enjoy!
